Questions answered

Still wondering? Reach out at support@teacherassist.ai

Do I need a credit card to start?
No. Your 5-day free trial starts immediately with no payment information required. You'll only be asked for payment details when you choose to subscribe after the trial.
What happens after the 5-day trial?
You'll be prompted to choose a plan. If you don't subscribe, your account becomes read-only — you can view past results but not run new grading sessions.
How does annual billing work?
Annual plans are billed once per year at the equivalent of 9 months' cost — you get 3 months free compared to monthly billing. Perfect for purchasing at the start of a school year.
Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?
Yes, anytime. Upgrades take effect immediately. Downgrades take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
What subjects does TeacherAssist.ai support?
All subjects. The AI grades based on your answer key and rubric, so it works for Social Studies, ELA, Science, Math, and anything else you teach.
Is my student data private?
Yes. Student work is processed only for grading and never used to train AI models. Each teacher's data is isolated and never shared. Student names are replaced with initials.
